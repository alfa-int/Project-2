Our idea :

Our idea with this travel agency is to allow the amateur sports club to be able to go on weekends or team building weeks with a given price. Activities, transport and accommodation is organized by us.

How to choose?

Choosing our agency means choosing to travel inexpensively, without having to organize anything. In the amateur clubs, no one takes care of being able to organize trips when it would do the greatest good for the clubs and the cohesion of the teams.

Calling on us means saving time, improving performance, pleasing and congratulating players as well as trainers and coaches.
